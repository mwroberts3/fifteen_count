Arcade

Tip: the best way to enjoy the Arcade mode is to play with only two general rules in mind, and then learn the other rules as you play. Card values must add up to 15 to submit, checking card or comboing cards that are the same color results in more points.

Phases

There are two phases of play that make up the core of the gameplay loop in Arcade Mode. The Count! phase involves tapping cards and trying to get a total that adds up to 15. The Combo! phase immediately follows and lets players sacrifice cards in order score points and shrink their hand.

Scoring Points

Pretty much every aspect of gameplay can result in more points. The bulk of points will generally be scored by checking cards in the Count! phase. Additional points can be scored in Combo! phase. Successfully playing the jackpot can also result in large points gains.

Adding Time

Time management is paramount to having a successful run. Time can be added through particular acheivements: checking at least half the cards in a players hand or checking all cards in a players hand.

Restoring Cards

A player can reset to a fullhand of 10 cards if they successfully check all the cards in their hand, which can only happen if the respective card values of each card adds up to 15.
