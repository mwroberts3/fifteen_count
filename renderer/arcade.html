Arcade

It looks like this is your first time playing Arcade. Here are the basics to get you going. (Full instructions can be accessed through the game menu)


You start with 10 cards of two different colors. Each card has a value as low as -1 and as high as 15.


The game moves in two phases: the Count! phase followed by the Combo! phase. In the count phase the goal is to count cards, (by clicking on them), to get a total value of 15. Once the count is up to 15. Press the Shift key (or click the submit button in the UI) to move on to the Combo! phase.


Cards that show 2 values can be selected by pressing [Shift] for the high value or [Ctrl] for the low value. (Controls can be changed in settings)

IMPORTANT! you can select the lower value for several cards with one click. Click all the multi-value cards you want to count first and then click [Ctrl] to count all the low values at once.

Jackpot cards will randomly pop up. Count them to rack up nice high scores!
