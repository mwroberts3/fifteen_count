How to Play Harmonia Time Attack

It looks like this is your first time playing.

The goal of Time Attack is to play as many cards as possible within the 180 second timer

Unlike Arcade mode there is no way to add more seconds to the clock. So all you have is 180 seconds. Make them count!

Scoring Points

There are two special types of cards in Time Attack: the bonus card and the bomb card.

Tips

Allow bomb cards are to be avoided generally, you can also use them to your advantage to clear a particularly tricky hand.

Try to play cards with high values as they come in. If you wait to long to play the high value cards you could run into a dead end and have to use a bomb card or restart the game.
