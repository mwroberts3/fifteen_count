First Time Basics

Looks like this is your first time playing. Like Arcade mode, to get points you must count cards that add up to 15.

Time Attack has a 180 second timer, and unlike Arcade mode, it's not possible to add more time to the clock. Cards come in a mix of two colors, with values from -1 to 12. Counting cards of the same color results in more points.

Additionally, there are two types of special cards. Bonus and Shuffle cards. The bonus card starts in the first position at the start of each round. You should try to get the bonus card in the final position, by submitting 15 count pairs in between its current position and the final position in the bottom right corner.

Bonus cards played from the final position will result in a points windfall.

A shuffle modifier appears on a random card each round, and if this card is played, your entire hand will be redealt and the bonus card's position will be reset.
