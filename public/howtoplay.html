Basics

Fifteen Count is a fast paced puzzle card game, where the goal is to get as many points as possible.

In Arcade mode play is broken down into two Rounds. The "Count Round" and the "Combo Round"

The "Count Round" asks players to check(click on) any number of cards, as long as their values add up to 15.

The "Combo Round" gives players the option of 'sacrificing' cards for additional points. Though player's hand's card count will permanantly lose the number of cards played in the combo round from their hand's maximum number of cards. For example, if you start out with ten cards and then play one in the Combo Round, the maximum number of cards you can have in your hand--going forward--will be nine. So, the Combo Round is great for adding to point totals, if used strategically.

Combos using at least half of the cards in your hand will add 7 seconds to the clock.
